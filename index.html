The kit (prototype concept)

A scrappy, science-forward prototype intended to make enzyme-based PET breakdown easier to understand and test in structured experiments.

What it is

A prototype workflow and materials bundle for exploring PETase-driven PET depolymerization in controlled test conditions.

Who it serves

Early backers, makers, and sustainability-minded learners who want to follow real prototype development in public.

What it enables

Hands-on learning around PETase, PET feedstock handling, and monomer pathway concepts (TPA + EG).

Science in plain terms

PETase is an enzyme associated with PET breakdown. In simplified terms, the goal is to break PET into reusable building blocks such as TPA and EG.

Step 1

Prepare PET feedstock and record baseline observations.

Step 2

Apply enzyme conditions and monitor progress.

Step 3

Evaluate outputs against PET → monomer pathways.
